Man who is god of cricket

Indian cricket player Sachin Tendulkar is considered one of the greatest batsmen in the history of his sport.

Moments of life:

  • 1973-born in Bombay, India
  • 1988- he scored 100 not out in for Bombay against Gujrat.
  • 1989-At the very young age of 16, Sachin played his first Test match against Pakistan in Karachi.
  • 1990-In the 1990 Test in England he scored a century at Old Trafford.
  • 1992 -During the 1991-1992 tour of Australia Tendulkar scored and unbeaten 148 in Sydney and another century on a bouncing pitch a Perth.
  • 1995-Sachin married Anjali, a doctor and the daughter of Gujarati industrialist Anand Mehta
  • 2003-In the 2003 Cricket World Cup, he made 673 runs in 11 matches which enabled India reach the final.
  • 2011-Tendulkar chalked up another milestone when he and his team propelled India to a World Cup victory over Sri Lanka, the first in his long career.
  • 2012-He retired from ODI competition in December
